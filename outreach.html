Branch Out!

Mount Olive as a community has about 30,000 people.

Yet, there are few volunteer organizations centered strictly in Mount Olive.

Therefore, as a community we should participate in random acts of kindness challenges at least once a week.

These challenges can include:

    1. Giving high fives.
    2. Opening the door for others.
    3. Writing nice compliments on a stranger's car.
    4. Sharing inspirational quotes to peers or random people,

    5.Helping someone carry heavy bags.
