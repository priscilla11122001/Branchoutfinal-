COMPOSTING

How to Compost

This can be done by anyone and in many places

    1. Get a bin to put your compost in
    2. Fill your bin with a layer of dirt (size of dirt depends on bin size)
    3. Next, fill your bin with a layer of your compost
    (food scrapes such as veggies scrapes)
    4. Then, alternate layers until your bin is filled
    5. Then, add worms to the top of the bin
    6. Lastly, put your compost bin in a location inside or outside your home
    * After about a month your new organic soil should be ready*

RECYCLING

Basics of Recycling

Some things you can do to recycle more is to
    1. Put cans, paper, and plastic in the recycling bin
    2. Cut out single-use plastics to reusable items
    3. Using a tote-bag instead of using plastic bags
    4. Giving your plastic bag to the retail store you brought it from,
    so they can reuse it

    Stores such as Walmart particapate in this activity!
    * Now that you learned the basic
    go out and make these changes in you and others lives*
